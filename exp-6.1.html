Few Bharat Ratna Awardees

Subhash Chandra Bose

Subhash Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist whose defiant patriotism made him a hero in India. He is known for his role in India's independence movement and for establishing the Indian National Army (INA).

Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan

Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan was an Indian philosopher and statesman who served as the first Vice President and the second President of India. He was a respected scholar and a prominent figure in Indian philosophy.

APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.
